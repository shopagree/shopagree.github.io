Take shopping back

Online commerce is rather solitary. Who can take shopping back to the community experience it was once intended to be?

Advice, approval, assistance

Shop/Agree uses our online communication platform for shoppers to seek advice, approval and assistance from friends, family and coworkers.

Decide, choose, pick

Shop/Agree uses different types of polls to enhance social commerce and foster feedback. Decide "yes or no" on your item, choose between multiple items or pick a size or color.

Direct communication

Shop/Agree communicates with you and your circle by pushing poll results, comment summaries and product links directly to your laptop and mobile.

Purchase landing page

Shop/Agree creates a landing page for each potential purchase. Your circle gets Facebook, text and email invitations to vote or comment on your purchase within 24 hours.

Custom contact links

Shop/Agree creates links for each contact, tracking participation and referring your friends to the so they can buy the product, or ask them to buy it for you.

Annonymous sharing

Shop/Agree gives you the option to anonymously share your shopping decision landing page polls and comments with retailers and manufacturers.

Works for everyone

Shop/Agree works for friends, kids, parents, coworkers, spouses, experts and other social dynamics. It's "going to the mall with your friends" for this digital era that also brings social participation back to buying for work.

Dear PollCart Community,

PollCart for business is entering the consumer-based world with the new Shop/Agree for consumers app. To this point, our target has been retailers who are seeking to add a novel, social approach to their checkout, but in August, we began the process of opening PollCart to every shopper with our new bridge to the API for consumers which we call “Shop/Agree.”

We have the resources to create a Chrome plugin for the Google browser and are building this first phase with our small team. We are seeking significant resources to enable us to transform Shop/Agree into a mobile or Facebook app, and are currently welcoming equity offers for a seed round from venture capital investors, to grow the social commerce platform to that next level. Please contact us if you’d like to get in on this exciting opportunity!

Shop/Agree extends the patented PollCart engagement from e-commerce business-to-business sales, to new possibilities for any consumer. All they need to do is install the Chrome extension or mobile app, log in to Facebook or visit shopagree.com, then ask some friends for input on their purchases. We anticipate utilizing data from this new consumer-facing product to make PollCart more compelling and relevant to retailers.

Shop/Agree works like this: A buyer selects contacts to poll about their online purchase. Then the contacts vote and comment on the potential purchase. Buyers can select their friends from Facebook, Google contacts or enter an email or mobile number for an invitation to participate. Once a participant agrees, they may vote, comment and review their contacts’ Shop/Agree purchases.

The key difference between PollCart’s retail application and the consumer-facing “Shop/Agree” is that Shop/Agree is not integrated with checkout the way PollCart is, thereby giving buyers the ability to use Shop/Agree at any online store. We’re making PollCart limitless for consumers and hope that doing so will give us the data we need to make PollCart more attractive and vital for new retail partners.
